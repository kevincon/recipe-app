Peanut-Butter and Jelly Sandwiches!

Ingredients

2 slices of bread!
2 tablespoons of Peanut Butter!
2 tablespoons of Jelly

Instructions

Take the two slices of bread and place them on a clean surface.
With a knife or spoon, spread peanut butter onto one slice of bread. If there is extra peanut butter, spread on other slice of bread
Add Jelly onto one slice of bread.
Combine the two slices of bread together to create a yummy tasty sandwich!
